About Limelight

A stage for
every night.

Tucked into the DDA Market of Paschim Vihar, Limelight with Sheesha is a premium café & lounge built for the moments that matter after dark — birthday tables pulled together, quiet couple evenings, and friends who never want the night to end. Expect delicious food, expertly crafted mocktails, premium sheesha and an ambience designed down to the last ember of warm light.
